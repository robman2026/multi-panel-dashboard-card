# Multi-Panel Dashboard Card

A Samsung Premium-style Home Assistant Lovelace card combining:

- 📷 **Live camera feeds** with tap-to-fullscreen
- 🔘 **Configurable switches grid** with state-aware icons and colors
- 📡 **Sensor rows** for doors, motion, lights, and more
- 🌡️ **Dual-ring climate gauges** (temp + humidity) with threshold-based colors
- 🧂 **Salt level gauge** with warning threshold

All sections, labels, icons, entities, columns, and color thresholds are fully configurable in the visual editor — no YAML required.

> Designed to match the Samsung Laundry Card style (Samsung Premium · Glassmorphism & Soft Glow).
